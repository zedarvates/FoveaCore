20 Tips I Wish I Knew When I Started
===

1. Always use a separate session for each reconstruction
2. Keep videos at 30fps maximum for best results
3. Use proper lighting: soft diffused light is best
4. Clean your sensor before recording
5. Move slowly around the object (30-60 seconds full rotation)
6. Avoid reflective surfaces
7. Keep your subject in frame at all times
8. Record in 1080p at minimum, 4K recommended
9. Don't zoom during recording
10. Avoid motion blur
11. Use manual exposure if possible
12. Keep consistent distance from subject
13. Check camera calibration first
14. Use mask preview before running full pipeline
15. Set threshold between 0.92 and 0.97 for most cases
16. Clean floaters before export
17. Enable anisotropic rendering for final results
18. Export after iteration 7000 for best balance
19. Save your session before closing
20. GPU memory is your bottleneck, not CPU
